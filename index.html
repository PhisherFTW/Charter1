Deep Sea Fishing Charters for Everyone

Our captain knows how to cater to any fisherman – from seasoned professionals to new customers who have never held a fishing rod on our deep sea fishing charters. Our fishing charters are great for kids and families as well! If you look at our photo gallery, you will see that we have a wide variety of customers and fish!

Wide Variety of Fishing Charter Experiences

Join us for a deep-sea fishing charter leaving Port Canaveral, near Cocoa Beach and Cape Canaveral, and experience hard pulling fish such as Grouper, Snapper, Sharks, and Amberjack. Try to tame the fast and furious Wahoo, Kingfish, and Barracuda. Enjoy the excitement of high jumping Sailfish, Tarpon, and Mahi. Bask in the thrill of site fishing for Cobia or Tripletail. Travel to the Gulf Stream’s backside in search of Yellowfin Tuna, Blue Marlin, and White Marlin. Explore the Continental Shelf’s depths by deep dropping for Swordfish, Tilefish, Snowy Grouper, and Yellow Edge Grouper.
Customers often ask us when the best time of year is to catch certain fish. We keep daily catch records every day that we fish. You can see past catches on our statistics page. We also have a fishing month-by-month infographic page that you can see what fish we are most likely to catch each month of the year.

Why choose us?

Sea Leveler has been in business, operating in Port Canaveral / Cape Canaveral near Cocoa Beach, since 2002, and our captains are among the best! We have all fished together since elementary school. Our captains are full-time captains; this is what we do every single day! Our captains are highly knowledgeable of all the fishing techniques needed for fishing out of the Cocoa Beach / Cape Canaveral area. They also know all of the latest regulations – both state and federal. Our captains are all certified by the US Coast Guard to operate fishing charters in the State of Florida.
Fishing is in our blood, and we can’t imagine doing anything else. We also talk to each other about the bite throughout the day and after each trip so that we can make sure you get the best experience possible!
